FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and services.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) as well as ACH bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our payment processor.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, we'll prorate the difference.

Is there a contract or long-term commitment?

No long-term contracts required. All plans are month-to-month, and you can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. Our annual plan offers a discount but is still cancelable with prorated refund.

What happens if I exceed my transaction limit?

If you exceed your monthly transaction limit, we'll automatically charge a small overage fee per additional transaction ($25 for Starter, $20 for Professional). Enterprise plans have no limits.

Do you offer discounts for teams?

Yes! We offer special team pricing for brokerages and teams with multiple agents. Contact our sales team for custom pricing based on your team size and needs.
